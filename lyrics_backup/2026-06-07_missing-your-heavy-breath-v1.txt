[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
The midnight shadows dance across the room
I breathe the memory of your sweet perfume
My hands are reaching across the empty bed
I wake up missing your heavy breath

[Verse 1]
We left the porch light burning in the rain
A quiet rhythm tapping on the pane
You wore my favorite jacket, holding tight
A perfect secret hidden in the night

[Pre-Chorus]
Now I'm just counting the miles until I'm home
It's way too hard to face the dark alone
I need the way you pull me in so close
You are the only magic that I know

[Chorus]
I want to hold you till the morning breaks
I want to feel the shiver that it makes
You leaning closer right against my chest
Oh, I keep missing your heavy breath

[Post-Chorus]
Missing the way you pull me in so deep
Missing your heavy breath before we sleep

[Verse 2]
I still remember how we parked the car
Beneath the blanket of a million stars
We didn't say a single word out loud
But heaven found a way to touch the ground

[Pre-Chorus 2]
Now the clock is ticking way too slow
The fire inside me is starting to grow
I need the way you pull me in so close

[Chorus]
I want to hold you till the morning breaks
I want to feel the shiver that it makes
You leaning closer right against my chest
Oh, I keep missing your heavy breath

[Post-Chorus 2]
Missing the way you pull me in so deep
Missing your heavy breath before we sleep

[Bridge]
The empty room is echoing the sound
Of all the quiet magic we have found
A burning feeling I can never hide
When you are lying right here by my side
You hear Saeed inside this melody
A longing crying out for you and me

[Spoken]
The room is quiet, but my heart is loud
I just want to feel your breath right now

[Final Chorus]
I want to hold you till the morning breaks
I want to feel the shiver that it makes
You leaning closer right against my chest
Forever missing your heavy breath
